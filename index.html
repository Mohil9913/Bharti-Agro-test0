Shivamrut Novel

'Shivamrut'

is Novel Organic mixture of Macro (N,P,K) and Micro ( Ca, Mg, S, Mn, Cu, Zn, Fe) Nutrients, Plant Growth Regulators (CK,NAA,GA),Urease activity, Total Phenol, and Various Bacterial Counts (TVC, Rhizobium, Azotobacter, PSB,KMB).It exhibits Novel effect on plant in terms of vigorous root development and vegetative growth, boom in flowering and maximum conversion of flowers in to fruits and pods, increase size and weight of fruit and reduce maturity by significant time.

Shivamrut Plus

Shivamrut Plus

is an organic Botanical Pesticide developed on the basis of latest biotechnology techniques by Navsari Agricultural University. It contains sap of various herbs that protects plant parts from damaging insects. It acts as Repellent and blocks the openings of breathing holes and suffocates the insects resulting in death of insects. This product is best fit for natural Agriculture. Shivamrut Plus is complete organic solution for crop as it protects plant from damaging insects in addition to vigorous vegetative growth, enhanced number of flowers and maximum conversion of flowers in to fruits and pods. Recommendation : it can be used on all crops like cereals, pulses,Fruits and vegetables.

Dosage : Foliar Spray : 10 ml per liter of water.



Shivamrut Prime

Shivamrut Prime

is an organic Botanical Fungicide developed on the basis of latest biotechnology techniques by Navsari Agricultural University. It contains sap of various herbs along with enriched organic liquid that protects the crops from various fungal diseases. This product is best fit for natural Agriculture. Shivamrut Prime is complete organic solution for crop as it protects plant from various diseases in addition to vigorous vegetative growth, enhanced number of flowers and maximum conversion of flowers in to fruits and pods. Recommendation : it can be used on all crops like cereals, pulses, Fruits and vegetables.

Dosage : Foliar Spray : 10 ml per liter of water.
